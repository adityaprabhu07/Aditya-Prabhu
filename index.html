Formula SAE

Illini Electric Motorsports - Aerodynamic Structures

Problem

  • Carbon-fiber front wing is hollow, so nothing can be mounted to it directly

Solution

  • Designed two structural insert variants: Structural Ribs on the leading edge and Screws as structural fasteners
  • Engineered inserts in Creo for mounting carbon-fiber front wing elements
  • Applied topology optimization to identify load paths and reduce material

Impact

  • Reduced weight by 25%+ over last season
  • Ongoing testing and validation
Relevant Skills: Creo CAD, Topology Optimization, ANSYS FEA

VEX U

Illini VEX Robotics - Chassis

Problem

  • Robot can be pushed sideways because of omni-directional wheels, reducing its defensive capabilities

Solution

  • Developed a custom 60A molded silicone traction wheel with a 3D-printed 3-piece mold
  • Engineered lightweight hub assembly and sawtooth tread pattern for performance
  • Optimized chassis design for weight reduction, improving robot agility and efficiency

Impact

  • Improved lateral resistance by 55%+ compared to OEM wheels
Relevant Skills: Fusion CAD, silicone molding

Smart Bin

Automated modular waste-sorting attachment

Problem

  • Waste contamination in schools
  • Existing smart-bins are costly and difficult to scale

Solution

  • Designed a modular attachment that fits on standard school trash cans using Fusion 360
  • Conducted topology optimization and used Voronoi patterns to reduce material cost without impacting rigidity
  • Developed an open-source platform for broader adoption and community contributions
  • Collaborated with NJIT Robotics and Sourland Conservancy for prototyping and outreach

Impact

  • Provided a low-cost, scalable design for sustainable waste management
  • Fostered collaboration between academic and conservation groups
Relevant Skills: Fusion 360 CAD, Topology Optimization, Sustainable Design

Hybrid Truck Fairing

Reducing Drag in Tractor–Trailer Gap

Problem

  • Tractor–trailer gap creates air vortices and turbulence, lowering efficiency
  • Current rigid fairings cannot fully cover gaps without limiting trailer turning

Solution

  • Created truck + trailer CAD models with pivoting trailer geometry
  • Designed hybrid fairing consisting of elastic fabric to cover the gap and telescoping rods with ball and socket joints for support
  • Ran CFD simulations in SimScale comparing control vs. hybrid model
  • Quick-deploy system: only 4 attachment points for installation/removal

Impact

  • Achieved 36% reduction in drag coefficient (0.01591 → 0.01168)
  • Proposed scalable solution to reduce fuel use and emissions in trucking industry while EVs are still in development
Relevant Skills: Fusion 360 CAD, Computational Fluid Dynamics (SimScale), Aerodynamics, Mechanical Design
